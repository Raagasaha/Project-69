School Library

Was built in 1980

This school library is available to hundreds of children. With thousands of different books, and computers with access to the internet, kids are able to spend their time learning more and researching. Additionally, our books are in high-quality, and we assure you that our books are clean, and available to read either online, or in a book.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

Built in 1982, this school auditorium is in fine condition. Kids learn the art of acting, and perform plays and musicals infront of audiences. Kids practice and enjoy special roles and jobs given in the play, so that every kid can contribute to performances.
